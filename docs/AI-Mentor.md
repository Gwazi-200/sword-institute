# AI Mentor Design

## Purpose
The AI Mentor is intended to support learners throughout their educational journey by providing guidance, encouragement, study support, and next-step recommendations. It is designed to complement human instruction rather than replace it.

## Core Objectives
The AI Mentor should:
- Help learners understand course concepts in clear language
- Suggest study plans and prioritized actions
- Encourage reflection and progress tracking
- Offer personalized learning support based on the learner’s current path
- Align recommendations with Sword Institute’s mission of social impact and responsible learning

## Planned Capabilities
- Course-related explanations and summaries
- Step-by-step learning support for assignments or projects
- Personalized recommendations for future learning paths
- Motivation and guidance for goal setting and completion
- Feedback prompts that encourage reflection and improvement

## User Experience
A learner should be able to interact with the AI Mentor from the dashboard or course view. The experience should feel supportive, practical, and easy to understand. Responses should remain concise, actionable, and context-aware.

## Design Principles
- Be helpful and encouraging
- Avoid over-automation in critical educational decisions
- Maintain clarity and accessibility in all responses
- Respect learner privacy and data boundaries
- Promote ethical and responsible use of AI

## Implementation Approach
The initial version can be implemented through a lightweight conversational interface connected to a backend service or intelligent prompt layer. Future versions can incorporate richer context from learner profiles, progress states, and course history.

## Success Metrics
- Learner engagement with mentor support increases
- Learners report improved clarity and confidence in their studies
- Completion rates improve for guided learning pathways
- AI responses are considered useful, safe, and relevant
